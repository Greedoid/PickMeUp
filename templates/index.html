Welcome to PickMeUp

We are your solution to a bad day

What is PickMeUp? It is an app that keeps track of your texts and performs sentiment analysis on them. If you're having a bad day, our service will be able to figure that out, and correspondingly will call a ride for you to the nearest venue that can give you a deal, where you can take the night off from your troubles before you face them head-on tomorrow.

How it works

The service will run in the background where it listens for when you need a friend. If it determines that you had a pretty shitty day, it will ask if you need a PickMeUp. If you say yes, you need only wait. If not, that's unfortunate, maybe next time, but in the mean time, cheer up bud. Stay Classy.
